Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

1kg Chicken breast sliced into cubes

1/2 kg plain yogurt

1pkt Shan Chicken Tikka Spice

2tbsp Lemon Juice

1 can diced tomato

2 tbsp Garlic crushed

1tbsp Jeera

1 Sliced Onion

1 cup fresh cream

Chopped coriander to garnish


Method:


1. Mix the chicken, yogurt, lemon juice, garlic and spice together.

2. Add diced can of tomatoes to the mixture.

3. Leave to marinate overnight.

4. Slice an onion and place in a pot to fry with Jeera.

5. When onions are lighlt brown, add marinated chicken and cook for 25 minutes

6. Add the cream and garnish with chooped coriander.
